Financial Assistance

Our dedicated financial advocacy programs provide a safety net for Royal Woods residents. We offer guidance on navigating local housing grants, utility bill relief for peak Texas summer months, and one-time emergency aid for unexpected hardships. Whether you need help with rent or staying connected, we are here to support your stability.

Health & Mental Health

Prioritize your well-being with our comprehensive health network. We connect you to low-cost medical clinics, confidential mental health counseling, and preventative wellness workshops. From managing chronic conditions to finding a supportive ear during stressful times, our resources ensure that every neighbor has access to the care they deserve.

Family Support

Building a stronger community starts at home. Our family services offer a wide range of support, including reliable childcare referrals, evidence-based parenting classes, and youth enrichment programs. We also provide specialized assistance for multi-generational households to ensure that both our children and seniors thrive together.
